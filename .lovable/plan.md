
# Simple TODO Application

A clean, minimal TODO app where you can add, complete, and delete tasks. Your tasks will be saved to the cloud so you can access them from any device.

---

## Authentication
- **Sign up / Sign in page** with email and password
- Clean, centered form matching the minimal aesthetic
- Automatic redirect to your task list after logging in
- Sign out option in the header

---

## Task Management
- **Add tasks** using a simple input field with an "Add" button
- **Mark tasks complete** by clicking a checkbox - completed tasks will show with a strikethrough
- **Delete tasks** with a trash icon button
- Tasks displayed in a clean, vertical list

---

## Design
- **Clean white background** with subtle shadows on cards
- **Minimal color palette** - primarily black/grey text with accent color for actions
- **Ample white space** for a calm, focused experience
- **Responsive layout** that works on both desktop and mobile

---

## Technical Setup
- **Lovable Cloud database** to store your tasks securely
- Each user's tasks are private and only visible to them
- Row-level security to protect your data
